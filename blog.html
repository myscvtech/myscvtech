What to Know Before Your Home Theater Installation

Planning a home theater or media room? Don’t make the mistake of buying a pre-packaged kit or guessing speaker placement. SCV Tech delivers clean, custom installations designed around your room and preferences.

  • Prewire Your Walls — Even if you’re not ready to add speakers yet, we recommend wiring during remodels or new builds to future-proof the room.
  • Strategic Mounting — Your TV’s placement should consider sightlines, glare, and seating height — not just wall space.
  • Hidden Equipment Racks — Store receivers, power units, and sources in a separate location with remote control access.
  • Room Acoustics — We help plan speaker locations, bass traps, and acoustic panels if needed for optimal sound.

Our team builds setups that look as good as they sound — and that are easy for anyone in your home to use.

Common AV Troubleshooting Mistakes and How to Fix Them

If your soundbar cuts out, your TV refuses to sync, or your smart devices seem to act up, chances are it’s not the equipment — it’s the setup.

  • Wrong Cables — Cheap or outdated HDMI/speaker wires cause weak audio, no signal, or limited features.
  • Skipped Calibration — TVs, subs, and surround speakers need adjustments to look and sound their best.
  • Overloaded Network — Streaming, security, and smart hubs need bandwidth — we optimize it.
  • Messy Wiring — Unlabeled cords and tangled cables make future troubleshooting harder and risk disconnections.

We diagnose issues fast and leave your system better than new. If it’s wired wrong, we’ll clean it up and make it right.
