About

At SCV Tech, we’re passionate about providing dependable and cutting-edge technology solutions for homes and businesses throughout the Santa Clarita Valley and beyond. With over 33 years of combined experience in the industry, our team brings professionalism, creativity, and attention to detail to every project.

From home theaters and network installations to advanced security systems and smart home automation, we treat every client’s space as if it were our own. Our reputation is built on trust, transparency, and long-term relationships.

Whether you're building new, upgrading, or troubleshooting tech — SCV Tech delivers custom solutions tailored to your lifestyle or business needs.

Our Service Areas

SCV Tech proudly serves residential and commercial clients within a 100-mile radius of Santa Clarita. We specialize in home theater, audio/video, lighting, smart home automation, security, and low-voltage services throughout Southern California.
